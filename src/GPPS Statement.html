Admissions Process:

To ensure a smooth and efficient admissions process, please follow these steps:

  1. Submission of Application: Download and complete the application form from our website or collect one from our school office. Submit the completed form along with the required documents (birth certificate, vaccination card, previous school reports, etc.) to our admissions office.
  2. Assessment and Interview: Once we receive your application, we will schedule an assessment and interview for your child. This will help us determine their academic level and ensure a smooth transition to our school.
  3. Offer of Admission: If your child is successful in their assessment and interview, we will offer them a place at our school. You will receive a letter outlining the terms and conditions of admission.
  4. Registration and Payment of Fees: To secure your child's place, please register them by paying the required fees and submitting all necessary documents.

Required Documents:

  • Birth certificate
  • Vaccination card
  • Previous school reports (if applicable)
  • Transfer letter (if applicable)
  • Passport-sized photographs (2)

Our fees structure is competitive and affordable. Please contact our admissions office for more information on our fees and payment plans.
